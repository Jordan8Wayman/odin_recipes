Cauliflower Alfredo Bake

Home

Description

Creamy, comforting, and deliciously wholesome, our Cauliflower Alfredo Bake reimagines a classic favorite with a lighter twist. Roasted cauliflower florets are smothered in a velvety, garlic-infused Alfredo sauce made from blended cauliflower and savory seasonings. Baked with layers of cheese until bubbly and golden, this dish delivers rich flavor and satisfying textureâ€”without the heaviness. Perfect as a hearty main or a decadent side.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cauliflower florets
  • 1 (15 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh peas
  • 4 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add cauliflower florets and cook for just 3 minutes. Drain.
  3. Transfer cauliflower to a greased 8x8-inch casserole dish. Pour Alfredo sauce over cauliflower. Sprinkle with peas and chopped bacon. Stir to combine.
  4. Bake uncovered until cauliflower is tender and sauce is bubbly, 25 to 30 minutes. Serve immediately.
